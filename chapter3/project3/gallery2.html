J. Paul Getty Museum

The J. Paul Getty Museum is one of the largest art museums in the world, specializing in works from the ancient world to the present day. The Getty Museum is known for its extensive collection of classical, Renaissance and Baroque art, as well as its cutting-edge exhibitions and public programs that promote the understanding of art and its role in history and culture.
