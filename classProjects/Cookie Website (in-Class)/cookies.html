Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy.
  4. Add egg and vanilla extract, beat until well combined.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to the wet mixture and mix until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips.
  7. Use a cookie scoop to drop dough onto a lined baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy your delicious peanut butter chocolate chip cookies!
